## Account Setup

### Creating Your Account

To create a new account:

1. Visit [https://example.com/signup](https://example.com/signup)
2. Enter your email address
3. Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Accept the terms of service
5. Click "Create Account"
6. **Check your email** for a verification link
7. Click the link to activate your account

**Note:** Verification links expire after 24 hours. If yours expired, use the resend link button.

### Verifying Your Email

After signing up, you'll receive a verification email.

1. Open the email from noreply@example.com
2. Click the blue "Verify Email" button
3. You'll be taken to a confirmation page
4. Your email is now verified

**Not receiving the email?**
- Check your spam folder
- Click "Resend Verification Email" on the signup page
- Make sure you entered the correct email address

### Setting Up Your Profile

Your profile helps other users know more about you and personalizes your experience.

**To update your profile:**

1. Click your avatar (top right corner)
2. Select "Profile Settings"
3. Update any of these fields:
   - **Profile Photo**: Click the camera icon to upload
   - **Full Name**: Your display name
   - **Bio**: A short description (optional)
   - **Location**: Your city/region (optional)
   - **Timezone**: For accurate timestamp display

4. Click "Save Changes"

### Changing Your Password

To change your password:

1. Go to Settings → Security
2. Click "Change Password"
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter your new password (must be different)
5. Confirm your new password
6. Click "Update Password"

**Password Requirements:**
- Minimum 8 characters
- Must contain at least one uppercase letter
- Must contain at least one number
- Must contain at least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

### Logging Out

To log out from your account:

1. Click your avatar (top right)
2. Select "Logout"
3. You'll be redirected to the login page

**Tip:** For security, always log out on shared computers.

## Basic Features

### Creating a Project

Projects are the main workspace for organizing your work.

**To create a new project:**

1. From your dashboard, click "+ New Project"
2. Choose an option:
   - **Blank Project** - Start from scratch
   - **Template Gallery** - Use a pre-made template
   - **Import** - Upload an existing project file
3. Enter a project name (required)
4. Add a description (optional)
5. Select privacy level:
   - **Private** - Only you can access
   - **Shared** - Select specific people
   - **Public** - Anyone with the link can view
6. Click "Create Project"

Your project will appear on your dashboard immediately.

### Inviting Team Members

To collaborate with others:

1. Open your project
2. Click the "Share" button (top right)
3. Enter team member email addresses
4. Select their permission level:
   - **Viewer** - View only, no changes
   - **Editor** - Can modify content
   - **Admin** - Full access including sharing
5. Click "Send Invitations"

Team members will receive an email invitation. They can accept and start collaborating.

### Using the Dashboard

Your dashboard is your home page. It shows:

- **Recent Projects** - Projects you've recently worked on
- **Shared with Me** - Projects others have shared
- **Suggestions** - Recommended projects based on your usage
- **Activity Feed** - Recent updates from your projects

**Dashboard Actions:**

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| Open project | Click on the project card |
| Create project | Click "+ New Project" button |
| Sort projects | Click "Sort" dropdown (top right) |
| Search | Use the search bar to find projects |
| Pin favorite | Click the star icon on any project |

## Advanced Features

Your file will download to your computer.

Access previous versions of your project.

**To view history:**

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Issue: Can't log in

**Symptoms:** "Invalid email or password" error

**Solutions:**
1. Verify you're using the correct email
2. Reset your password:
   - Go to login page
   - Click "Forgot Password"
   - Enter your email
   - Follow the link in your email
3. Clear browser cookies and try again
4. Use a different browser

**Still having issues?** Contact support: support@example.com

#### Issue: Slow Performance

**Symptoms:** Page loading slowly, freezing, or timing out

**Solutions:**
1. Refresh your browser (Ctrl + R)
2. Clear browser cache:
   - Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data
   - Firefox: Options → Privacy → Clear data
3. Disable browser extensions temporarily
4. Try a different browser
5. Check your internet connection speed

**Still having issues?** Check our [status page](https://status.example.com) for service updates.

#### Issue: Can't share projects

**Symptoms:** Share button is grayed out or invitation doesn't send

**Solutions:**
1. Make sure you're the project owner
2. Check the email addresses are correct
3. Verify the user hasn't been already invited
4. Try refreshing the page
5. Logout and login again

#### Issue: Missing Data

**Symptoms:** Some data disappeared or is not showing

**Solutions:**
1. Check project's version history (may have been accidentally deleted)
2. If it's in version history, restore from there
3. Try accessing from a different browser/device
4. Clear browser cache

**Important:** If data is permanently deleted, we cannot recover it. Always keep backups.
